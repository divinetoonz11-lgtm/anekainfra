Our Interior Execution Process

At Aneka Interior, we follow a structured and transparent execution process to ensure timely delivery, cost control, and premium quality across residential, commercial, and corporate interior projects.

01

Site Visit & Requirement Analysis

Our team visits the site to understand your space, usage, expectations, budget, and timeline. This forms the foundation of the entire project.

02

Design Development & Planning

Based on requirements, we develop layout plans, design concepts, and working drawings aligned with functionality, aesthetics, and feasibility.

03

Material Selection & Final Estimation

Materials, finishes, and specifications are finalized with transparent costing. Final BOQ and timelines are frozen before execution begins.

04

Execution & Site Supervision

Dedicated project managers ensure quality workmanship, coordination with vendors, and adherence to approved designs and schedules.

05

Quality Check & Handover

Final inspection, snag resolution, and systematic handover ensure a ready-to-use space delivered as committed.
