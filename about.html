About Aneka Infra
Aneka Infra Pvt Ltd is an integrated construction and infrastructure development company delivering high-quality residential, commercial, and urban infrastructure projects across India.

With a strong foundation in engineering excellence, disciplined execution, and long-term value creation, Aneka Infra has successfully completed multiple civil construction projects and continues to expand its footprint across construction, interiors, and digital real estate services.

The group also operates a dedicated interior design division focused on premium residential and commercial interiors, and a technology-driven real estate listing platform connecting buyers, sellers, and developers.
